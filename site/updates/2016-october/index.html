October 2016 UpdateSeveral New Features

1View multiple floors

  • View up to 4 floors simultaneously!  The default is based on your screen size, but can be changed in the top right menu.
  • Tip:Clicking/tapping will display a pink marker on all floors that can help you idenitfy what is directly above/below a location.

2Room label styles

  • Choose from light, dark, and large combos!

3Camera line of sights

  • Cameras now have line of sight markings indicating their coverage.
  • Tip:Hovering over a camera highlights its line of sight.  You can also adjust the starting opacity in the top right menu.

4Select map grid

  • Now shows when you first arrive at r6maps.com.  Toggle it by clicking/tapping the top left logo.

5German translations

  • German translations are ready for all room names and most of the site!
  • Want more?I am interested in supporting other languages if others can help enter room names (~5-10 hour committment).  Please contact me if you are interested in helping.

Plus more ...and what's next

  • MenuCheck out the top right menu for other new features. Options selected are saved per device).

  • What's next?In a few weeks, beta test a real-time team collaboration session feature in order to determine how useful it could be.
