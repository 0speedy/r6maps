Octover 2016 Update Several New Features

1View multiple floors

  • View up to 4 floors simultaneously!
  • Default is based on your screen size, and can be adjusted in the top right Menu.
  • Click/tap to show a ping marker on all floors.  This can help you identify what is directly above/below a location (e.g. to find the best spot to fuze a camper from).

2Room label styles

  • Choose from light, dark, and large combos!

3Camera line of sights

  • Cameras now have line of sight lines. As an attacker this might help you sneak around without necessarily destroying a camera.
  • You can hover over a camera to better see its line of sights, or adjust the starting opacity in the top right Menu.

4Select map grid

  • Shows when you first arrive at r6maps.com.  Get back to it by clicking/tapping the top left logo.
  • Automatically adjusts itself to fit your screen. :)

5German translations

  • In addition to English and French!
  • I am interested in supporting other languages if others can help enter room names (~6-12 hour committment).  Please contact me if you are interested.
